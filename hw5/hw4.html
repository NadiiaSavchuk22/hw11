List of musicians

Musicians:

  • Adele Is a British singer-songwriter known for her deep, emotional voice. She has won numerous awards, including multiple Grammy Awards, and has captured the hearts of millions worldwide.
  • Skryabin A Ukrainian musician, composer, and songwriter, famous in the Ukrainian rock scene. His music blends rock, pop, and folk. After his tragic death in 2015, he left a lasting impact on Ukrainian music.
  • Ed Sheeran Born on February 17, 1991, in Halifax, England. British singer, songwriter, and musician. He started his career in 2005, gaining popularity through online videos and songs. His debut album "Plus" was released in 2011. He is one of the most successful pop artists worldwide.
